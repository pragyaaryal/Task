Introducing "Mamu ko Swadh",

which translates to mom's taste.

We are a small business that values quality and love in every product we make. Our journey began two years back when Kamala Mamu started selling homemade delicacies at a farmers market. We're now a homebased local Nepali business selling authentic taste of nepali spices in the form of Achar, Massyaura, Titaura, Gundruk, Chop, etc. We focus on providing quality products made with love.

Lapsi ko Achar

Made with hogplum, sugar, chilly, spices, cardamom and more

Made with Radish, spices, and more

Masala ko Achar

Made with Spices, Nibuwa, oil, and more

Made with round chillies, spices, oil, and more
